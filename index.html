About

Hello! I’m Molly Hajimoradi. I work on data, machine learning, and analytics solutions that turn complex datasets into actionable insights. My experience includes building automated pipelines, forecasting models, and AI-driven analytics to support decision-making in logistics, transportation, and operations.

I’m currently completing my MASc in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I focus on large-scale transportation systems and advanced analytical methods. I also bring several years of industry experience in manufacturing analytics, with a strong interest in scalable, collaborative, and impact-driven data solutions.
